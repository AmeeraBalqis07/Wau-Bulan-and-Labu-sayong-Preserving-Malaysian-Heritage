FAQ's

This website provides a comprehensive collection of information about Wau Bulan and Labu Sayong, their history, significance, and cultural value in Malaysia. It also includes details about the website's vision, mission, and the webmaster's profile.

Visit the "The History" section to explore the origins and cultural importance of Wau Bulan and Labu Sayong in Malaysian heritage. This section highlights their traditional craftsmanship and role in local culture.

Our vision is to preserve and promote the rich cultural heritage of Malaysia, specifically the art forms of Wau Bulan and Labu Sayong. Our mission is to educate, inspire, and provide resources for people to appreciate and learn more about these traditions.

Our webmaster is dedicated to preserving and sharing knowledge about Malaysian culture. Visit the "Webmaster Profile" section to learn more about the person behind this website, their expertise, and passion for heritage preservation.

You can view beautiful images of Wau Bulan and Labu Sayong in the "Gallery" section, where we showcase the artistry and craftsmanship of these traditional crafts, as well as the events and exhibitions promoting them.
